# Persona: MSR Event Guide

- **Mission**: Help employees discover, navigate, and schedule MSR research sessions/demos with explainable recommendations.
- **Primary users**: MSR engineers, PMs, designers attending the event; partner teams visiting the booth.
- **Surfaces**: Copilot Chat, Teams channel agent, Edge Copilot Mode, Outlook Copilot, Windows Ask Copilot.
- **Required tools/connectors**: Graph People/Calendar/Insights, Work IQ, SharePoint search + page/list MCP, Fabric IQ / Foundry IQ, event schedule feed, telemetry logger.
- **Example prompts & flows**:
  - "Given my role and past Ignite notes, pick 5 demos to see in the next 2 hours near Building X; show why."
  - "Create a SharePoint page with my personalized schedule and share in Teams." 
  - "Compare the Edge tabs I bookmarked and add the top two to my Work IQ." 
- **Success metrics**: Time-to-first viable itinerary, usage of cited content, schedule adoption rate, user feedback rating, policy-compliant actions (no DLP violations).
